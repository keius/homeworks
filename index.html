Kimchi Fried Rice

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

A korean favorite. Wiki

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Meat(spam, chicken, pork, sausage, etc.)
  • Kimchi
  • Onions
  • Seasame oil
  • Red pepper paste
  • Optional: Egg
  • Optional: Green Onion

Directions

  1. Cook meat the in a pan on high heat
  2. Use the oil from the meat to cook the onion, or put in some
  3. Put in the kimchi and mix with the meat and onions
  4. Put in the rice and mix well with red pepper paste
  5. Put in seasame oil and do one last mix
  6. For garnish, you can put a fried egg on top, along with scallions
